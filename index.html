Psychoacoustic enhancer and bass processor

AURALIFT 32

A multi-band processing unit: it adds high harmonics (more detail, sparkle and clarity), deepens and boosts the bass, and widens the stereo image, all in a single, coherently tuned tool. Rather than boosting frequency bands like a standard equaliser, the AURALIFT 32 adds new harmonic and bass content, much like an analogue exciter, hence the effect of "opening up" the mix.

Mix

Your music is your story. You need to tell it as well as possible.

We understand your needs perfectly, which is why we work in a hybrid workflow, combining the precision of digital tools with the warmth of an analogue mastering chain. We deliver the finished mix + mastering within a maximum of 72 hours of receiving the complete set of files.

FAQ

How much does it cost?
The starting price is $41 + TAX for 5 stems. Each additional 5 stems costs an extra $15. Mastering only (without mixing) costs $50 + TAX per track.
How many revisions are included in the price?
The package price includes 5 revisions. Each additional set of 5 revisions costs $25 + TAX. In practice, we usually require between 1 and 3 revisions.
How should I prepare the files?
Export all tracks as separate 24-bit WAV files, without effects (unless they are part of the production). Each file should start at the beginning of the song.
Just mastering without mixing?
Yes, you can order mastering only, at $50 + TAX per track.
